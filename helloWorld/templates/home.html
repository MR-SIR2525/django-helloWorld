About my Django app

I created this Django web app during my time at Pearl River Community College as a way to practice my web development/Django skills while simultaneously finishing my full-stack web dev homework. Because I like cars, I decided to use information about them to practice and demonstrate database CRUD interaction (Create, Read, Update, and Delete). You can interact with my car data with the navigation bar at the top.
